## Strategy Tips

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Post daily or near-daily | Post once and wait |
| Always attach a video/GIF | Post text-only |
| Tag the tools you used | Tag random accounts |
| CTA in replies only | CTA in main post |
| Quote trending AI news | Ignore trends |
| Repost others who use your prompts | Only post your own |
| Use Screen Studio / CleanShot for smooth recordings | Use raw screen recordings |

---

## Recording Your Site for Twitter

1. **Screen Studio** (Mac) — best quality, hover effects, smooth cursor
2. **CleanShot X** (Mac) — good alternative, free trial
3. **Shots.so** — wrap your recording in a device mockup:
   - Upload to [shots.so](https://shots.so)
   - Pick an **animated** template
   - Choose a background (or use one of your generated backgrounds)
   - Export as MP4 or GIF
   - ~10% visual quality boost — worth doing
